Who We Are

Born from a Passion for Freshness & Quality

Meat Street & Spice was founded with one simple belief: every family deserves access to the freshest, cleanest, and most ethically sourced poultry — without the hassle of visiting a busy market.

We started small, sourcing from trusted local farms, perfecting our double-washing process, and crafting our signature spice blends in-house. Today, we're proud to be Karachi's go-to premium poultry delivery brand, trusted by hundreds of households every week.

  • Sourced from healthy, farm-raised, hormone-free poultry.
  • Expert butchers ensuring perfect cuts every time.
  • Authentic gourmet spice blends crafted in-house.
  • Delivered fresh in temperature-controlled packaging.

How We Prepare Your Order

1

Farm Sourcing

We partner with local, ethical farms that raise healthy, hormone-free poultry in humane conditions — ensuring premium quality from the very beginning of the chain.

2

Halal Slaughter

Every bird is slaughtered strictly following Halal guidelines by certified practitioners — ensuring full religious compliance and ethical, humane processing.

3

Expert Cutting & Cleaning

Our expert butchers perform a double-wash and precision cuts tailored to your order — from whole birds to boneless cubes, fillets, and marinated packs.

4

Sealed & Delivered

Packed in food-grade trays, sealed inside insulated thermal bags with ice packs — your order arrives at peak freshness, ready to cook, right at your door.
