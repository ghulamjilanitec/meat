Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

What areas in Karachi do you deliver to?

We currently deliver across most of Karachi, including DHA, Clifton, Gulshan-e-Iqbal, North Nazimabad, PECHS, Saddar, Bahadurabad, and surrounding areas. If you're unsure whether we cover your area, simply drop us a WhatsApp message and we'll confirm instantly.

How long does delivery take after placing an order?

Standard delivery takes 2–4 hours after order confirmation. For same-day delivery, orders must be placed before 6:00 PM. We also offer scheduled delivery slots — you can specify your preferred time when placing your order.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept Cash on Delivery (COD), Bank Transfer, JazzCash, EasyPaisa, NayaPay, and major Credit/Debit Cards. All payment methods are secure and verified before dispatch.

Is there a minimum order quantity?

Yes, our minimum order is 1 KG per product. However, you can mix and match different cuts and marinades in a single order — for example, 0.5 KG boneless cubes and 0.5 KG tikka marination. This gives you maximum flexibility.

Can I request a custom cut or marination?

Absolutely! We specialize in custom cuts tailored to your needs. Whether it's a specific size, bone-in vs boneless preference, or a custom spice blend, simply mention it in your order note or WhatsApp us. We love accommodating special requests.

How do you ensure freshness during delivery?

Every order is sealed in food-grade, airtight trays immediately after cutting. These are then packed into insulated thermal bags alongside frozen ice packs — maintaining a safe temperature throughout the entire delivery journey to your doorstep.
